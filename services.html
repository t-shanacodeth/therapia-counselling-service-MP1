What is psychotherapy?

Psychotherapy, also referred to as psychotherapeutic counselling is a talk therapy. This involves talking through the emotional difficulties and challenges that you are facing with a trained therapist.

What to Expect

CONSULTATION

We will get to know each other a little bit. We will discuss the challenges that you are facing and what you expect from therapy.

FIRST SESSION

The first session is an assessment session, which will include some information and forms.
This is to ensure that you get the best service possible and from me and to get to know each other.
It's important for you to feel that we are a good fit, and questions are always welcome.

FOLLOWING SESSIONS

We can start to dig deeper into your challenges at a pace that suits you, through open and honest conversations.

REFLECTIVE SESSIONS

The sixth session of therapy (six-week mark) is a reflective session.
It gives you the chance to look back on your past sessions and think about what you liked, what you haven't liked, and what you might like to focus on more in your future therapy sessions. This is to ensure that you are getting the most of your sessions.
