ONLINE INDIVIDUAL PSYCHOTHERAPY

What is psychotherapy?


Psychotherapy, often referred to as 'talk therapy', is a collaborative treatment designed to help individuals understand and manage emotional, psychological, and behavioral challenges. Through regular sessions, clients work with a trained therapist to explore their thoughts, feelings, and patterns of behavior in a safe, confidential setting.

The goal of psychotherapy is to help clients gain insight into their problems, develop healthier coping strategies, and improve their overall well-being. Depending on individual needs various therapeutic approaches may be used, such as: cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), psychodynamic therapy, or person-centered therapy. Psychotherapy can be effective for addressing issues like anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship difficulties, and stress.

By fostering self-awareness and offering guidance, psychotherapy supports personal growth, emotional healing, and positive life changes.


Modalities


PERSON-CENTRED THERAPY


Person-centred therapy is a non-directive, empathetic approach that empowers clients to explore their thoughts and emotions in a safe, supportive environment. By offering empathy, authenticity, and unconditional positive regard, the therapist helps clients access their own strengths, fostering self-discovery, personal growth, and emotional healing. This approach is ideal for those seeking greater self-awareness and improved well-being.


EXISTENTIAL THERAPY


Existential therapy is a philosophical approach to counselling that focuses on exploring life’s fundamental questions, such as meaning, freedom, responsibility, and mortality. Rather than focusing solely on symptoms, existential therapy helps clients confront the deeper issues of existence, guiding them to live more authentically and align their lives with their values.

In this form of therapy, clients are encouraged to take ownership of their choices and embrace personal freedom, while also acknowledging the uncertainties and challenges of life. By reflecting on their existence, clients can discover purpose and make meaningful decisions that improve their sense of fulfillment and well-being.

Existential therapy is particularly effective for individuals struggling with anxiety, depression, life transitions, or a lack of direction. It empowers clients to face life’s uncertainties with courage and create meaning in their own lives.


What to Expect


CONSULTATION


We will get to know each other a little bit. We will discuss the challenges that you are facing and what you expect from therapy.


FIRST SESSION


The first session is an assessment session, which will include some information and forms. This is to ensure that you get the best service possible and from me and to get to know each other. It's important for you to feel that we are a good fit, and questions are always welcome.


FOLLOWING SESSIONS


We can start to dig deeper into your challenges at a pace that suits you, through open and honest conversations.


REFLECTIVE SESSIONS


The sixth session of therapy is a reflective session.
It gives you the chance to look back on your past sessions and think about what you liked, what you haven't liked, and what you might like to focus on more in your future therapy sessions. This is to ensure that you are getting the most of your sessions.
