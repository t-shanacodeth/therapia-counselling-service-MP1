Life Changing Benefits of Therapy

Therapy is a lot of work. But through honest and vulnerable conversations with a trained therapist, and action, you can come out the other side of challenging issues and find peace.

Healthy Boundaries

Feel confident being able to identify and set boundaries, whether personal or interpersonal - crucial to maintaining identify.

Increase Self-Confidence

Feel empowered in the ability to advocate for yourself and the decisions that you make.

Increase Self-Awareness

Gain clarity and understanding of your inner self and how your negative feelings and thought patterns manifest in your behaviours.

Heal & Strengthen Relationships

Learn how to problem solve and resolve conflicts to build or maintain better relationships with your colleagues or loved ones.

Improve Communication Skills

Learn how to clearly communicate your expectations and actively listen to those around you to create a strong foundation of trust.

Develop Coping Skills & Strategies

Put coping skills in place to help reduce your emotional distress and effectively deal with stressful situations in the long run instead of seeking unhealthy quick fixes.

Hi! I'm Natalie, a Counsellor & Psychotherapist

image
Natalie Atandare
MBACP, ACTO, ACC

I am an experienced person-centred counsellor and psychotherapist who strives to be approachable, warm and accepting.

I walk alongside adults from diverse backgrounds, supporting them in their times of difficulty and pain in a SAFE, confidential and non-judgemental space.

My focus is to understand the challenges surrounding the circumstances in a person's life, and help them adapt to the changes whilst increasing their feelings of SELF-WORTH.

I will be there to help you reflect on every step of your healing journey, enabling you to GROW from your struggles, HEAL from your pain, and move forward to a place of quiet and mental calm.

  • Cultural challenges

Initially I was apprehensive about seeking therapy as I have always struggled with sharing details of my life with anyone and the thought of doing this with a complete stranger was daunting. This was important as I began to face the core of some deep-rooted pain. Natalie was alongside me through it all, creating a space without judgement where I could be free to speak honestly, and it was through her gentle, attentive manner and skill at being able to link things I had said weeks or months before that supported me to process particularly difficult events in my life and have a deeper understanding of myself. Natalie's ability to hear what is said, and all of what is unsaid, and make sense of it all, even when it's not clearly articulated is a skill to behold.
