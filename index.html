Hi! I'm Natalie, a Counsellor & Psychotherapist

image
Natalie Atandare
MBACP, ACTO, ACC

I am an experienced person-centred counsellor and psychotherapist who strives to be approachable, warm and accepting.

I walk alongside adults from diverse backgrounds, supporting them in their times of difficulty and pain in a SAFE, confidential and non-judgemental space.

My focus is to understand the challenges surrounding the circumstances in a person's life, and help them adapt to the changes whilst increasing their feelings of SELF-WORTH.

I will be there to help you reflect on every step of your healing journey, enabling you to GROW from your struggles, HEAL from your pain, and move forward to a place of quiet and mental calm.
